Frequently Asked Questions

What is Answer Engine Optimization?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing your content to be featured in AI-powered search results from tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

How is AEO different from SEO?

While SEO focuses on ranking in traditional search engines, AEO focuses on being cited by AI systems that directly answer user questions.

Why is structured data important?

Structured data helps AI systems understand your content better, leading to more accurate citations and improved visibility.
